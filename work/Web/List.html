STEAK FRIES

HOME            LIST           

  • INGREDIENTS

    • 3 Potatoes
    • Olive Oil
    • salt and pepper

  • Preparation

    1. Start Preheating Oven 475 degrees
    2. Wash and dry the Potatoes
    3. Cut Lengthwise 1/2 inch thick sticks
    4. get a large sheet pan and drizzle generous amounts of olive oil on it
    5. place the sheet pan with oil in the oven for 5 minutes
    6. Remove the pan and place the potato slices on the sheet in a single layer
    7. Salt and pepper The potatoes
    8. Return the sheet with fries and bake for 15 minutes
    9. Bring out the sheet to rotate the fries
    10. bake for another 15 minutes
    11. serve with steak otherwise it would not be steak fries.
